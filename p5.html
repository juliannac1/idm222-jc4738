Moon Girl

Her true self emerges under the glow of the moon
The subtle gleam of lunar beauty
She is not worthy enough for the sun
So she seeks comfort in the dead of night
As she reaches for the stars,
Her footsteps dance in sync with the wind
Her spirit
Her grace
Her pained and aggrieved soul
She whispers secrets to the daisies as they fall asleep
All she ever wanted was to be heard
The moon holds her hand when she’s lost
And for a moment
This young girl is holding the sky
Together,
They are one
Together,
They are beautiful
She is the moon’s girl
