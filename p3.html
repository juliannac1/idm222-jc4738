Beginning of the End

Why did you make me?
Why did you make me?
I am built like twig I am not steel
I am not a brick of steel
I am not your girl.
Your one,
I am not your one.
My eyes are dilated and the pale under my skin is reaching for the surface
Like a baby that’s been drowning too long and had her first breathe
She reached the surface and she’s breathing now but I just look pale
And dark
And sad
My eyes are black now and dilated,
I just pulled my hair up, so hopefully I look better than I feel as I go outside
On my fifth walk of the day searching for the door to my freedom
I walk towards the light because I think it’s leading me to Heaven
Or a safe place
A quiet place where I can breathe a little easier than usual and my eyes are the color of hazelnut, like they used to be
It’s strange, isn’t it?
Mistaking the sun for heaven
I like to go in front of the mirror and check if my heartbeat still looks like it’s beating and if my eyes look tired again today
But once again it is and my eyes still look tired
And dilated
So I regret checking
I laugh so hard when people see me cry and ask me if im doing fine. Not because it’s funny but because, well, it is funny actually. My chest is pounding and I’m holding my breath looking for a way out, but there’s nothing in my sight.
I try and rub my key against my skin so it feels like I’m being cut and bled alive
Like I wish I was
How did it happen again?
