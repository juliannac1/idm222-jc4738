People Aren’t Homes

i could never seem to replace the people who have left my life with new ones.
new people, I mean.
it didn’t matter how much time I spend with them or
how much love and admiration I have for them
these new people only fill a section of me that has never been explored before
a section I have never felt in my body before
its an unfamiliar feeling, yes.
as for the people who have left me?
or those I had to leave?
they’ve clasped onto my life and
they exist in every hole inside of me
perhaps, they’re more like rips,
like a piece of fabric
but I see them in every piece of song lyric I hear or
the most random things I see
the more someone leaves,
the more susceptible I am to topple
and bottle a gallon of bleach
but in return of such circumstance
i am able to learn a different way of hanging on
to make sure that I do not lose my balance
and end up standing in front of a loaded gun.
i am a piece of rope and I’ve just attached myself to a snapped tree branch
its time to untie myself now
and run.
run far far away
i am a piece of fabric
that is edging for its collapse
simply touching it can cause a commotion
now I’m not a monster,
i will visit these holes every now and then to pay my respects but
that’s all.
for it is not safe to poke this hole now
it is not safe to try to hang from a snapping tree branch.
